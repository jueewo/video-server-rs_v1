║  🎯 WALL-BASED VIDEO ORIENTATION FIX                          ║
╚════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

🐛 PROBLEM:
   Videos on different walls had inconsistent orientation:
   • North wall: ✅ Correct
   • South wall: ❌ Mirrored (left-right flipped)
   • East wall:  ✅ Correct
   • West wall:  ✅ Correct

🔍 ROOT CAUSE:
   South wall is rotated 180° (π radians) to face inward.
   When we removed the blanket horizontal flip, South wall
   videos became mirrored because they NEED the flip to
   compensate for the wall's 180° rotation.

✅ SOLUTION:
   Detect wall rotation and flip texture ONLY for 180° walls:
   
   const isRotated180 = 
     Math.abs(rotation.y - Math.PI) < 0.1;
   
   videoTexture.uScale = isRotated180 ? -1 : 1;
   posterTexture.uScale = isRotated180 ? -1 : 1;

📊 RESULTS:
   After Fix:
   • North wall (0°):   ✅ Correct (no flip)
   • South wall (180°): ✅ Correct (flipped)
   • East wall (90°):   ✅ Correct (no flip)
   • West wall (-90°):  ✅ Correct (no flip)
